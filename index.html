New vs Repeat ConsultantsiNew: a firm whose first-ever contract in the filtered dataset falls in that year — i.e. a firm no city in this dataset had previously awarded work to.

Repeat: a firm that received a contract in an earlier year and is receiving another one now. Once a firm is "new" in a given year, it's counted as "repeat" for every subsequent year.

Each contract is counted (not each unique firm), so a firm with multiple contracts in its debut year adds multiple to "new".
